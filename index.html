Biography

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the University of Munich on Machine Learning, advised by Prof. Volker Tresp. During my PhD, I also spend time conducting research in Siemens Corporate Technology on Explainable AI. My research interests include computer vision and machine learning. Especially, I am interested in the following topics:

  • Explainability and Robustness of Deep Learning
  • Semi/Self-supervised Representation Learning
  • Image Understanding with Capsule Networks, Graph Neural Networks
