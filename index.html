Biography

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the University of Munich on Machine Learning, advised by Prof. Volker Tresp. My research interests include computer vision and machine learning. Especially, I am interested in the following topics:
  • Robustness and Explainability of Deep Learning
  • Image Understanding with Cognitive Models (e.g. Capsule Networks and Transformers)
  • Model Compression via Knowledge Distillation
  • Semi/Self-supervised Representation Learning
