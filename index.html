Biography

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the University of Munich on Deep Learning, advised by Prof. Volker Tresp. My research interests include computer vision and deep learning. Especially, I am interested in the following topics:

  • Visual Question Answering using Scene Graph Reasoning
  • Image and Object Understanding with Cognitive Models (e.g. Transformers)
